"Economics of Science: Looking at Turkish Authors around the World."

- with Akcigit Research Team
We use the Prescribed Medicine and Household Consolidated files from the Medical Expenditure Panel Survey 2014-2019 to analyze trends and factors associated with the prescribing of pharmacogenomic medications. Using a novel dataset on medications with known pharmacogenomic associations, we identify persons with at least one pharmacogenomic prescription event and use this group as our primary sub-population for analysis.
